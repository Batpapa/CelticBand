About “The Rocky Road to Dublin”

1 contributor

This song, like many recorded by The Dubliners, is a traditional Irish song recorded by many artists. It tells the too-common tale of a young Irishman who leaves home to go to England for a job and better living conditions and the difficulties he encounters on the way.
